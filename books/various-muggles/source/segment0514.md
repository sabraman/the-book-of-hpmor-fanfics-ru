# Chapter 1

Harry had planned to ask her again for the green light on his latest project (he thought he had worked out how to make a wizarding Internet that wouldn't destroy the world), but then she came over and casually sat on his lap.

"What are you doing?" he asked instead, and not very fluently.

"I'm not kissing you" said Hermione.

He gulped.

"I can see that. But what *are* you doing?"

She furrowed her brow and bit her lip.

"Not kissing you is all that I'm currently doing"

"You are sitting on my lap- and a negative action is not an action. A negative action is nothing"

"OK, then: I'm *actively* choosing not to kiss you. Right now"

"Ah"

There was a silence. It was increasingly obvious for Harry that she wasn't going anywhere until whatever it was she expected happened. He sighed. heavily.

"So, you are taking advantage of my hormone-addled brain for some reason"

She nodded absently, not even looking at him.

"Could you tell me what you want, so that we can get it over with?"

"I'm not in a hurry"

They fell in a comfortable silence. Confortable, that is, for her.

"So..." he started- only to be cheerfully interrupted.

"Why can't you stop thinking of me kissing you?"

"I'm not- I can-" he huffed, but it was too late: he was already blushing. "Because you keep mentioning it!" It was basic psychology: hearing 'don't think of an elephant' will make you think of an elephant. "Have you been reading my books?"

"You know I have: you've been practically forcing them on me"

*Maybe that's the problem*, thought Harry. *Then again, the continued existence of the world needs Hermione to be as rational and informed as possible.*

"I don't mind it, Harry" she said, misinterpreting his thoughtfulness. "At all"

"I know, I know. Is this revenge, anyway? for..."

"For what?" she asked, smiling.

"I don't know. You definitely weren't this evil last year. Did I corrupt you?"

She choked on a laugh.

"You're much cuter inside than you give yourself credit for, *Tom*: too cute to corrupt anybody"

"Then what's the matter?"

"Well... I've been feeling like this for almost a year now, and you're only starting to catch up"

"You are talking about... puberty?"

Hermione nodded and licked her lips. Her head was nearly as red as Harry's now, but it was *gracefully* red. Harry had never managed to factor her supernatural gracefulness out when thinking about her, and he wasn't sure he wanted to... but he knew his friend enough that unicorn magic couldn't mask her insecurities from him (although it made them seem graceful themselves somehow). She needed him now.

"Hermione..."

"Yes?"

"I don't think I'm ready for that..."

"If you're worried about me having preconceptions on how love is supposed to look like, don't. I'm too old for that"

"Yeah. You're what, thirteen?" full power sarcasm didn't stop her though: she was right, and they both knew it.

"What I mean is, I've been around you too much. Aaaand I have a time machine. Anyway, you want my mouth on yours, and if *your* preconceptions are going to stop you from enjoying it..."

"Are you going to kiss me then?"

"What makes you think that?" Her tone of voice had become playful- in a graceful way, of course. "You said 'no kissing', and I myself said at the beginning of this conversation that I was deciding *not* to kiss you. More importantly, I'm not done messing with you"

 

 

You said no kissing: Aftermath

*You guys should see yourselves right now*, said Ravenclaw. He hadn't been very vocal for a while: he was having too much fun watching the situation play out, and well, nobody would listen to him anyway.

*Let's go for it*, said Gryffindor, not for the first time.

*And play right into her hands?*, argued Slytherin, if a little weakly. *We need to make her initiate it.*

*That's why you're a lonely bastard*, said Hufflepuff, *Hermione Granger's hands are perfectly safe to play right into.*

*They're lovely hands*, agreed the main Harry Potter, and then he kissed Hermione already.
