General points first:

- Relative advantages: partial transfiguration, dementor control, general scientific knowledge, and the ability to think of strategies which involve doing nice things for other people
- Harry cannot speak magical pseudo-latin without being shot at. His only established wordless magic is transfiguration.
- In order to have some actual chance at success, we should try to keep the number of steps below three, so trying to obtain time turner/cloak/other objects is probably not viable. The one thing Harry might be able to summon by thought alone is a phoenix, but I wouldn't count on it.
- Hermione is a juggernaut.
- Even a short-term solution better dovetail into a long-term solution to the whole Voldemort problem VERY QUICKLY.
- If magical resonance begins between Harry and Voldemort, Harry will fall over screaming in pain, which will presumably prompt the Death Eaters to open fire. However, their standing orders are not to kill.
- Voldemort will not directly attack with magic.

Approach:

Let's step back and look at the Voldemort immortality problem before jumping into the immediate problem. We know that Voldemort's stream of consciousness is constantly syncing to the cloud of horcruxes. The horCloud itself would be extremely difficult to disable. However, if the stream of consciousness can be corrupted, then the corruption should instantly sync through the whole horCloud and Voldemort should be removed as a threat. The simplest and most reliable way to do this would be to severely damage Voldemort's brain (without killing him, since he may need to be conscious for the stream of consciousness to sync). It would take careful thinking to maximize damage while minimizing the chance that Voldemort has some safety mechanism in place, but the bottom line is that Voldemort can probably be removed as a long-term threat as long as Harry can subdue Voldemort and call the cavalry.

Let's assume talking our way out is a bust. At best we might trick Voldemort into something stupid, and any attempt whatsoever to trick Voldemort is a crapshoot. We probably can't convince Voldemort to put off killing Harry, what with the world at stake. Likewise, we probably can't convince Voldemort that the prophecy was not about Harry (he'd probably kill Harry just to be on the safe side anyway).

So we're looking for some sort of transfiguration which can disable and/or kill the death eaters, and disable but NOT kill Voldemort. Transfiguring air failed in chapter 28, but that was pre-partial-transfiguration, and it seems very likely that partial transfiguration will work on air. At least one of air, surface of wand, dust, or Harry's hand is sufficient for transfigurable materiel.

To deal with the Death Eaters, transfiguring a toxic or highly corrosive gas (e.g. serin or sulfuric acid vapor) or drug vapor (e.g. aerosolized rohypnol) should suffice. Very little material would be necessary, so Harry should be able to transfigure it quickly and easily. Delivery mechanism is an issue. If Harry does manage to transfigure air, then he might be able to exploit his recent shaping exercises to have the gas appear in tiny clouds around the Death Eaters' faces. Otherwise, he'll just have to start by transfiguring a very thin shield or bubble around himself (probably a good idea anyway, especially if he uses serin).

Voldemort is trickier. As soon as Voldemort interacts with Harry's transfigured materiel, it should establish resonance. (Even if the resonance isn't triggered by Harry's transfiguration, Voldemort will presumably react to a faceful of nasty by counterspelling, which will DEFINITELY trigger resonance). That resonance is most likely to incapacitate both of them, and Voldemort will be out for quite some time. That's a very good outcome ASSUMING the Death Eaters are already out of the game. So the trickiest part of all this is timing. The Death Eaters need to be out before Harry sends any transfigured gas toward Voldemort. Serin is probably best for this purpose, since it is colorless, odorless, and can kill in 60 seconds (plus Harry can probably stall by telling Voldemort about dementors). It does have the unfortunate side effect of probably killing Lucius Malfoy if he's present, but it's a faster and safer bet than any hallucinogens or anesthetics I know. Alternatively, if Harry can make the gas appear right in front of the Death Eaters rather than having it diffuse, then just using sulfuric acid vapor against everyone more or less simultaneously should be viable and nonlethal as long as first aid shows up quickly.

Note that Hermione is basically invincible, so none of this should permanently harm her regardless.

Summary of plan:

1. Quick test whether air can be transfigured via partial transfiguration and, if so, whether air can be transfigured at a distance via shaping.
2a. If air can be transfigured at a distance, create small bursts of sulfuric acid vapor around each Death Eater's face and wand hand. Create a burst slightly farther away for Voldemort, to leave time so that the Death Eaters are well-damaged before resonance starts.
2b. If air cannot be transfigured at a distance, transfigure a minimal bubble, just enough to form a seal, and then transfigure serin gas outside the bubble. Use air/hand/wand/dust specks as transfigurable materiel. Delay as long as needed by talking about dementors, and add additional membranes as needed to make sure gas reaches Death Eaters well before Voldemort. Send a transfiguration toward Voldemort as soon as Death Eaters show signs of trouble.
3. Drop the transfiguration as soon as resonance starts. Definitely do not want to breath that stuff.
4. Resonance starts, and hopefully the Death Eaters are dealt with. If the Death Eaters are not fully dealt with, hopefully Hermione wakes up once the resonance starts and can do some damage while Harry and Voldemort are incapacitated. Even if the Death Eaters are ok, there's a good chance they'll turn on Voldemort while he's down.
5. Call the cavalry, tell them not to interrupt the quidditch game because time, and let Snape/Moody come up with creative cognitive modifications to be uploaded to the horCloud.

That's it. A bit complicated, but mostly the likely-to-fail parts have backups.
