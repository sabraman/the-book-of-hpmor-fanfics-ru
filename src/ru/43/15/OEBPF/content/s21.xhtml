Chapter 21: Lampshading in Blue

Author's Note: The next portion of Hermione and Twilight's journey was supposed be told in Graphic Novel form, with a few pages being released in between each written chapter. In total, the chapter was going to be roughly 15-20 graphic novel-sized pages. But then after I finished the first two pages (which were more artwork than story), I realized that telling a narrative via graphic novel is hard. Who would have thought? So here are the final pages of my aborted attempt, hanging a Draconequus-sized lampshade on the whole affair.
