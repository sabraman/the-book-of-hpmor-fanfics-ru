For the latter, he had allowed Harry's existence, but for the former, he has decided to destroy Harry in response to a credible prophetic threat.

Voldemort expects to understand perfectly when the prophecy has been fulfilled or denied, since he was the direct recipient of the prophecy. However, even after the Unbreakable Vow, Voldemort still is not reassured of the outcome. So he falls back to the next plan which has occurred to him: destroy Harry. However, acting before fully and perfectly comprehending the riddle of a prophecy can bind one to an undesired future.
